Welcome to my blog. Here I will share insights from my experience as a General Practitioner specialising in lifestyle medicine.

Expect articles on:

- **Lifestyle medicine fundamentals** — the evidence behind nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress management, and social connection as medicine
- **Clinical practice** — practical tips and case studies from general practice
- **Medical education** — updates on my free courses and teaching resources
- **Patient empowerment** — how to take an active role in your own health

I believe that lifestyle medicine has the power to transform how we approach chronic disease. Through this blog, I hope to make that knowledge accessible to both healthcare professionals and patients.

Stay tuned for more posts.
