## Background

- Medical training and qualifications

- Professional memberships and affiliations

## Why These Courses?

Dr Jayant Karambhe created these free, open-source courses to make high-quality lifestyle medicine education accessible to healthcare professionals and patients alike. Each course is designed to be practical, evidence-based, and easy to follow.
